This study is conducted to classify and detect the nutritional deficiencies in coffee plants. Four varieties of coffee plants are included in the study; Arabica, Liberica, Excelsa and Robusta. Eight nutritional deficiencies are identified in Robusta, these are Nitrogen (N), Potassium (K), Phosphorus (P), Calcium (Ca), Boron (B), Zinc (Z), Magnesium (Mg), and Iron (Fe). Phosphorus (P) and Potassium (K) are found in Arabica variety. Phosphorus (P) and Nitrogen (N) in Liberica variety. Excelsa variety has deficiencies in Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K) and Calcium (Ca). Temperature readings from the coffee farms and nurseries are also identified. The standard temperature reading for Arabica is from 15 degree Celsius to 24 degree Celsius. The standard temperature reading for Liberica, Excelsa and Robusta is from 24 degree Celsius to 30 degree Celsius
